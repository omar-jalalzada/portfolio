Eats, a mobile app

Eats helps you easily share pictures of the food you're eating with your friends while keeping tracking of your eating habits and spendings.

Whenever you get the munchies, Eats can also act as a visual menu for your local eateries curated by your friends.

I designed Eats to:
  • Share your food and interact with friends.
  • Keep track of what you eat and how much you spend on food.
  • Explore food in the area curated by friends.
  • Stay motivated to eat better, healthier and cheaper.
