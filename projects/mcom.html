Brief details and Project Conditions

  • 1) Provide both actionable (short term) and strategic (longer term) recommendations
  • 2) Craft contextual experiences for key sections within each application
  • 3) Adhere to the technical infrastructure of existing legacy systems
Timeline
3 week design sprint incorporating stakeholder engagements and ideation sessions
Team
Omar Jalalzada — UI Lead Pauly Ting — UX Lead
Tools
Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop, Omnigraffle and Invision App

The Process

  • 1) Understand & Relate

    • Humanizing our efforts by re-framing the problems and asking - "What do people really want?"
    • Understanding the context by doing a brain-dump of m-commerce and real-world retail experiences
    • Honing our focus by identifying 'pain points' to explore - content digestion, path to store, content relevancy

  • 2) Research & Analyze

    • Conducting in-person interviews with subject matter experts and users
    • Distilled and analyzed key themes and patterns to drive functionality
    • Created user-personas and user-story work flows

  • 3) Design, Prototype & Iterate

    • Defined a clear path to product based on the user-story
    • Iterated through lo-fi wireframes for each individual user story
    • Built an interactive app prototype to test functionalities on the device
